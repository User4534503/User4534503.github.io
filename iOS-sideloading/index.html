How to Sideload on iOS

To sideload apps onto your iOS device, follow these steps:

  • Download a sideloading tool like AltStore or Sideloadly.
  • Install the sideloading tool on your computer.
  • Connect your iOS device to your computer and use the sideloading tool to install the app.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to ensure successful installation.
