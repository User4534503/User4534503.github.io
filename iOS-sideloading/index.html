The Ultimate Guide to Sideloading on iOS

What is Sideloading?

Sideloading is the process of installing apps on your iOS device from sources outside the App Store. This can be useful for testing, using apps not approved by Apple, or accessing older app versions.

Why Sideload?

  • Access apps not available on the App Store.
  • Install beta versions of apps.
  • Bypass regional restrictions.

Requirements for Sideloading

Before you start, you'll need:

  • A computer (Mac or Windows)
  • An Apple ID
  • A sideloading tool (like AltStore or Sideloadly)

Getting Started

To begin sideloading, you'll first need to download and set up a sideloading tool. The process varies depending on the tool you choose.
